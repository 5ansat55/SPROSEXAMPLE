About Us

Know Us

SPROS is an eco-friendly education, consultancy institution founded by experts who have gained experience by conducting project and sustainable planning management on education, trade, environment and construction issues for many years. SPROS is a professional based scientific organization.

In particular, SPROS develops new solutions and new technology to design and implement new methods for current social environment problems by using the research results of scientific information and technology to provide planning and sustaining projects.

Furhermore

SPROS establishes the framework for current and future social needs by establishing links to information transfer between public and private business. SPROS's portfolio of expert and academic staff and contracted consultants have all the necessary professional qualifications to provide solutions to the problems all mentioned.

Academicians and experts with international education, experience and knowledge provide all the necessary expertise with their experience in projects by expanding the framework of scientific action and perspectives on the projects.

Missions

SPROS carries out national and international programs in a sustainable manner with the application of highly professional and innovative educational projects by protecting the traditional values of society with its expertise and innovative structure.

SPROS has been strengthening running projects with experts who provide innovative professional solutions to the social problems by experience in social, academic, engineering and agriculture for many years.

SPROS has determined to produce alternative solutions related to Environmental Science and Technology, Ecology and Agriculture in the fields of research and innovation and in rural and urban areas with many social activities focusing on knowledge and experience.
