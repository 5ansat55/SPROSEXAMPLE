INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS

SPROS also has a wide range of interaction between international partners. SPROS has a wide network of local and international public institutions, companies, municipalities and education organisations. SPROS cooperate with civil society organizations such as vocational and technical coordination center employees, schools in Turkey, EU countries as well in all over the world, SPROS has an active project and training network with Bulgaria, Spain, Portugual, Belarus, Sweden, Macedonia, Malta, France, Germany, the Netherlands, Belgium, Italy and the Czech Republic. SPROS is a strong solution partner that supports the partnership between international institutions. SPROS serves support services and project development with professional services for all projects carried out in Turkey and the EU countries on the establishment of management and partnership with experienced professionals. SPROS has been involved in many national and international projects for more than 20 years.
